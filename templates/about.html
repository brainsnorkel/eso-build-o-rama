About ESOBuild.com

ESOBuild.com is an up-to-date report of "meta" Elder Scrolls Online trial builds, automatically curated from real ESO Logs data. It lets you explore effective and popular builds used by top-performing players for each trial and boss encounter. In short, it answers the question: "what is meta right now?" with real data from top players.

Built by @brainsnorkel (PC NA). Let me know in game mail if you find it useful, or open an issue on the GitHub issues tracker, if you find a problem.

How It Works

To this tool, a "build" is a unique combination of subclasses (skill lines), and two "5-piece" gear sets with 4 or more pieces of each equipped. This is a simplifying assumption, and other heuristics may be added later (come back 5/2/2/2/1 builds!).

ESO Build scans the top-ranked trial reports (with logs) from ESO Logs, analyzing player builds in each boss fight to identify the most frequent gear and subclass combinations: the meta. This might not pick the highest DPS (they might have a unique build) but it will find common builds. ESOBuild.com is better at finding DPS builds than healers and tanks because of their lower numbers and proliferation of subclass options. However, esologs.com links are provided so you can explore group composition at esologs.com and dig out those unique builds.

Build Requirements

A build is considered "common" and included in our report if it appears:

  • 5 or more times for DPS builds
  • 2 or more times for healer and tank builds

This ensures we only showcase builds that have been seen in multiple successful, top-ranked, trial runs.

Data Source

All build data comes from ESO Logs, the premier logging platform for Elder Scrolls Online. We analyze the 10 top ranked reports for each trial to ensure we're capturing the most successful common builds.

Updates

Builds are updated daily, with trials processed one at a time.
