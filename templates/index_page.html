About This Data

This page shows the most popular builds from the top 10 ranked logs for each trial boss. A build is considered "common" and featured here if it appears 5+ times (DPS) or 3+ times (Tank/Healer) among top players.

Build Definition: A unique combination of subclasses (skill lines) and two 5-piece gear sets.

Data Source: ESO Logs public rankings, automatically scanned weekly.
